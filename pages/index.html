History of Computer Science

1940s: The Birth of Computers

The first electronic computers were developed during World War II, marking the beginning of computer science as a field.

1960s: The Rise of Programming Languages

High-level programming languages like FORTRAN and COBOL emerged, making programming more accessible.

1980s: Personal Computers

The introduction of personal computers revolutionized the way people interacted with technology.

2000s: The Internet Age

The internet became widely accessible, leading to the development of web technologies and online services.

2020s: AI and Beyond

Artificial intelligence and machine learning are at the forefront of technological advancements, shaping the future of computer science.
